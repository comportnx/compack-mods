# COMPACK Mods

Mods included in this pack:

### Tough Tyres

`Wheels are more resistant to punctures` `Armoured and 'wide off-road' wheels are immune to punctures`

Wheels are much more resistant to punctures, while armoured wheels and off-road wide wheels are immune to them (found on military vehicles, tractors, excavators, and trenchers).

---

### Fast Pulping

`Pulping corpses is much faster`

This mod has the effect of making pulping zombie corpses much faster, though special tools (prying etc) are still needed for larger corpses.

---

### Reduced Fungal Radius

`Fungal bloom spawn radius reduced`

This mod keeps fungal monsters much closer to their blooms, shrinking the area of the overmap they dominate.
